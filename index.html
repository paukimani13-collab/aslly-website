Who We Are

ASLLY CONTRACTORS LIMITED is a fully registered Road, Building and Water Construction company with the National Construction Authority (NCA) and Ministry of Roads & Public Works. All directors are proud Kenyan citizens. Our operational base is in Eldoret, Kenya.

We deliver high-quality construction, transport contracts, plant hire and general supplies across Kenya and are expanding into East Africa.
