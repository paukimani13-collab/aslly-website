About ASLLY CONTRACTORS LIMITED

Proudly Kenyan • NCA Registered • Delivering Excellence Since Day One

Our Story

ASLLY CONTRACTORS LIMITED is a fully registered Road, Building and Water Construction company under the Companies Act, the Ministry of Roads and Public Works, and the National Construction Authority (NCA). All directors are Kenyan citizens. Our operational base is in Eldoret, Kenya.

We work across public and private sectors, undertake transport contracts, hire out machines and equipment, and supply building/construction materials. We also engage in general supplies including office stationery, medical equipment, and promotional advertising.
